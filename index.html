Features

Everything You Need to Guide Stress-Free

From automatic booking collection to analytics, JustGuide handles the admin so you can focus on your guests.

Aggregate Automatically

Connect any booking source — Guruwalk, FreeTour, GetYourGuide, Viator, TripAdvisor, your website, even manual sources. JustGuide collects them all into one unified dashboard.

Mobile-First Dashboard

Your booking list, designed for your phone. Mark attendance, add notes, manage tours on the go. Everything at your fingertips when you need it.

One-Click Messages

Send automatic pre-tour messages via WhatsApp or SMS. Your guests always know where to meet. No more no-shows or confused travelers.

Money & Analytics

Track earnings month by month, tour by tour. See which platforms perform best and optimize your schedule based on real data.

Platform Notifications

Never miss a change. Get notified instantly about cancellations, updates, and new bookings across all your connected platforms.

Agency Mode

Running a team? Dispatch tours to guides in one click. Maintain clear schedules and shared analytics. No more coordination headaches.
